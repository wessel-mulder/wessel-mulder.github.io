September 2024 – Present

University of Adelaide / University of Copenhagen (Joint Program)

PhD fellow under supervision of Damien Fordham, Carsten Rahbek, and Kasper Thorup.

My PhD research focuses on using process-based modelling to identify species traits that promote resilience in the face of global change. I study how climate and land-use change have affected European bird populations in recent decades, and how species traits mediate changes in the distributions of these birds. This work aims to untangle the mechanisms underpinning species responses to climate and land-use change and offer perspective on the future of avian persistence under global change.
